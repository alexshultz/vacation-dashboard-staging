**

[Grand Country Music Hall](/viewer/place?mid=/m/0lr0ty8&sa=X&ved=2ahUKEwizj7S23_iTAxUQGtAFHR6EJNkQqdYPegYIAQgGEAE)

** 

Known for having the densest daily schedule with shows from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM. 

- **[Grand Jubilee](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attractions-g44160-Activities-c58-t111-Branson_Missouri.html)**: A fast-paced quartet variety show featuring "New South".
- **[Comedy Jamboree](https://tickets.grandcountry.com/show/comedy-jamboree/2026-05-22)**: Afternoon variety featuring eccentric comedy and Ozarks-style family fun.
- **[Down Home Country](https://www.branson.com/shows/)**: Morning classics for fans of traditional country hits.
- **New South Gospel**: A dedicated Southern Gospel performance featuring powerful four-part harmonies.
- **Ozarks Country**: Sunday evening traditional country and gospel.
- **[Ozarks Gospel](https://www.bransonmissouri.com/shows-in-branson-missouri/duttons/tickets/2026-11-30_19%3A30%3A00/)**: A spiritual afternoon of hymns and stories.
- **Amazing Pets**: Morning variety featuring talented dogs, cats, and exotic birds.
- **Pets & Giggles**: An interactive variety show designed specifically for young children.

- **Motown Downtown**: Soulful tributes to Stevie Wonder, Marvin Gaye, and The Temptations.
- **[Best of Dean Martin](https://www.tix.com/ticket-sales/bransonhothits/3350/event/1455510)**: The "King of Cool" brought to life through his signature hits.
- **[Back To Country With Mark Aldred](https://www.facebook.com/sightsoundtheatreslancaster/posts/dont-miss-your-shot-at-the-final-season-of-david-in-branson-opening-march-7th-bo/1331468072340488/)**: A 1960s-70s country tribute honoring the foundations of the genre.
- **Doo Wop & More**: A nostalgic vocal journey back to the 50s and 60s.

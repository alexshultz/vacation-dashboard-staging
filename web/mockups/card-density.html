Branson '26 · Mockup · Card Density Study

Two card densities, same attractions

Alex is exploring two different card densities — one for browsing a big catalog of 100+ activities (where sparse is king), and one for wishlist items where you're deciding for real. Scroll through both sections and notice: do you process the catalog cards faster? Does the wishlist card have everything you'd want to commit? What's missing? What's noise?

What's new this round: wishlist cards now use TWO buttons (I'm In / Not Going) instead of three. Tap to select, tap again to deselect (back to undecided). The 🗑️ icon in the corner moves an item to a trash list (recoverable for 60 days). There's no Heart button on wishlist cards anymore — everything here is already wishlisted.

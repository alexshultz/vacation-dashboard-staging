Quick guide for family members.

🗓️

What is this?

This is the Branson 2026 family vacation planner — a private website just for our trip. Use it to browse activities and shows, save the ones you're excited about, and see what everyone else is interested in. No login required.

👤

Setting your name

Tap the profile button (👤) in the top-right corner of any page. Pick your name from the list. This tells the app who you are so your hearts and picks are saved under your name. You can change it any time.

♡

The wishlist heart

On any activity or show card, tap the heart (♡) to save it to your wishlist. It turns solid (♥) when saved. See everything you've hearted on the Wishlist page.

🎴

Quick Pick

Quick Pick lets you swipe through cards fast. Swipe right (or tap ✓) to add something to your wishlist. Swipe left (or tap ✗) to skip it. It's the fastest way to go through everything and tell us what you want to do.

👁️

Who sees your picks?

For now, everyone in the family can see everyone's wishlist. This helps us plan together. In a future update (Phase 2) we'll add the option to keep your picks private until you're ready to share.

Help

How to use the Branson '26 dashboard.

Get started

First thing: set your name. Tap the person icon in the top-right corner (or go to Profile in the menu) and pick your name from the list. The dashboard remembers you and saves your picks automatically.

Activities

Tap Activities to browse everything to do in Branson. Use the chips at the top to filter by type (shows, outdoors, food, and more). Tap the heart on any card to save it to your Wishlist.

Quick Pick

A fast way to sort through activities without reading every one. Swipe right (or tap ✓) if you're interested — swipe left (or tap ✕) to skip. You'll find it via the Quick Pick button inside Activities.

Wishlist

Everything you've hearted shows up here. Tap the heart again to remove something. You can also see other family members' wishlists — a great way to find out what everyone wants to do.

Suggested

The dashboard looks at the interests you set in Profile and suggests activities you haven't seen yet. Set your interests in Profile to get better suggestions.

Timeline

See what's scheduled for each day of the trip. Tap a day to expand it. Great for spotting which days are already packed.

People

Shows when each family member is in Branson. If you set your arrival and departure dates in Profile, you'll show up on this chart too.

Profile

Your settings home base. Set your name, pick a color theme (there are 14 to choose from!), choose light or dark mode, set your trip dates, and choose your interests. Everything saves automatically — no sign-in needed.

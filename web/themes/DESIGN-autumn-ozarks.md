## Overview

The Ozarks in October are genuinely spectacular. Autumn uses deep burgundy and burnt orange to capture the feeling of maple ridgelines at peak color. Rich, warm, and slightly dramatic. The harvest cream background feels like afternoon light through colored leaves.

## Colors

- **Primary (#7B1F3C):** Deep burgundy. The dominant maple color at peak fall.
- **Secondary (#9B4C0A):** Burnt orange. Ridge-line oak trees and late afternoon light.
- **Tertiary (#4A2D7A):** Autumn twilight purple. Committed events in harvest season.
- **Surface (#FFF8F0 / #FFF0DC):** Harvest cream. Warm and rich.
- **Accent sand (#C8980A):** Autumn gold. Ginkgo and sweetgum at peak color.
- **Accent clay (#8B3A2A):** Deep clay-brown. Remove and danger actions.

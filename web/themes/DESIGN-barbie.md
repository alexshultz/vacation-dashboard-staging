## Overview

Dreamhouse pink walls, Ken's blue corvette, and Barbie's golden hair. The Barbie theme uses THE iconic Barbie hot pink (Pantone 219 C) as the primary action color. Ken blue handles wishlist states. Glam purple marks committed events. Light mode is inside the Dreamhouse -- bright and glamorous. Dark mode is the opening scene of the 2023 movie -- deep magenta-black Barbieland sky.

- **Surface (#FFF8FC / #FFF0F8):** Dreamhouse pink-white. Every wall dripping glamour.

## Overview

A clear July 4th afternoon -- bunting on the porches, the parade on Main Street. Patriotic uses exact US flag colors (Old Glory Red #B22234, Old Glory Blue #3C3B6E) with Declaration of Independence parchment backgrounds. Light mode is a sunny Independence Day parade. Dark mode is fireworks at midnight on the 250th anniversary -- gold bursts, red fire, blue-white stars against a deep midnight sky.

- **Primary (#1A6B1A):** Patriot green -- confirmed going!

- **Tertiary (#7A6000):** Muted firework gold -- locked and booked.
- **Surface (#FDFAF5 / #F5F0E8):** Declaration parchment warm white -- 250 years of dignity.
- **Accent sand (#9A6A00):** Colonial gold -- darkened parchment warmth.

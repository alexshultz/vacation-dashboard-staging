# Resale/Ticketing Sites to Deprioritize for Branson 2026 Research

These sites primarily sell secondary/resale tickets and often have dynamic pricing, limited details on durations, official images, or accurate show info. Ignore them as primary sources. Use only for availability checks as a last resort. Prefer official theater websites, branson.com, or direct box office pages for logos, descriptions, prices, and thumbnails.

**List to ignore:**
- viagogo.com
- vividseats.com
- stubhub.com
- anyticket.com
- seatgeek.com
- gametime.co
- bransonshowticketsdirect.com
- tix.com
- eventticketscenter.com

**Preferred sources (examples from our current data):**
- 1hitsofthe60s.com (Hits of the 60s)
- ripleys.com/branson (Ripley's)
- cleancomedyhypnotist.com (Aaron Wayne)
- tickets.petersenband.com (The Petersens)
- branson.com/shows/* (many official listings like Grand Jubilee, Legends in Concert, Clay Cooper)
- Individual theater sites (americana-theatre.com, grandcountry.com, kingscastletheatre.com, hamners.com, sightandsound.com, etc.)

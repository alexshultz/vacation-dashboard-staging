## Overview

The structured confidence of Airtable's design system. Vivid primary blue with a full spectrum of status colors — green, pink-red, purple, and orange. Clean white and light-blue backgrounds keep the multi-color palette from overwhelming. Friendly and organized without being childish.

## Typography

Inter (Google Fonts). Slightly heavier label weight (500) reflects Airtable's UI-dense field labels.

## Light Mode

Clean white cards on pale blue-tinted background. Multi-color status chips pop against neutral surfaces. Blue primary actions clearly distinguish interactive from passive elements.

## Dark Mode

Deep blue-tinted dark backgrounds (H=215 formula). Multi-color accents brighten to maintain contrast. Status colors remain distinct and legible.

## Best For

Structured itinerary planning, table-like views, users who like clear color-coded status, families who want organized and bright.

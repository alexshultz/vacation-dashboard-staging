## Overview

The confident clarity of Wise's fintech design language. Deep navy ink on clean white with a vivid lime-green accent that's surprising but not harsh. Friendly and modern -- feels trustworthy without being corporate. The green is the star; everything else supports it.

## Typography

Inter (Google Fonts) with slightly heavier label weight (500) -- Wise's UI uses medium-weight labels for clarity.

## Light Mode

Clean white cards on near-white background with subtle green tint. Navy primary actions feel confident and clear. Lime green accent pops on secondary actions and highlights. High contrast, low decoration.

## Dark Mode

Deep navy-tinted dark backgrounds (H=200 formula). Lime green maintains excellent contrast against dark surfaces -- this theme actually improves in dark mode. Teal tertiary brightens slightly.

## Best For

Modern-feeling preference, users who like the unexpected lime accent, clarity-first itinerary planning, anyone who likes Wise/Revolut design language.

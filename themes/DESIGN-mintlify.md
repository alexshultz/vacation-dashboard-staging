## Overview

The clean green-accented aesthetic of Mintlify's documentation platform. Crisp white backgrounds, emerald-green primary actions, and precise typography hierarchy. Content-forward and distraction-free -- every element earns its space on the page.

## Typography

Inter (Google Fonts). Slightly looser line-height on body (1.6) for comfortable long-form reading -- Mintlify is a docs platform, so reading comfort matters.

## Light Mode

Crisp white cards on near-white background. Emerald green for primary interactive elements only -- no decorative green. Precise border lines keep structure clear without visual noise.

## Dark Mode

Deep cool-gray dark backgrounds (H=160 formula). Emerald green brightens to maintain contrast. Feels like a modern dark-mode documentation site.

## Best For

Content-heavy pages (help, schedule, timeline), focused readers, clean minimalist preference, users who plan carefully.

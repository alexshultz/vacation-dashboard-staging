## Overview

The warm minimalism of Notion's document-first aesthetic. Off-white backgrounds, deep warm near-black ink, and a deliberately restrained accent palette. Feels like a well-organized planning document — calm, readable, focused. Small pops of blue for actions, red for danger, and green for confirmation.

## Typography

Inter (Google Fonts). Notion's actual font is very similar to Inter. Slightly lighter weight hierarchy than other themes -- comfort reading over heroic display.

## Light Mode

Warm off-white page background with pure-white cards. Deep warm-gray ink, subtle grid lines. Blue for primary interactive states, not for the page chrome. Very low visual noise -- content-first.

## Dark Mode

Dark warm-gray backgrounds (H=35 formula). White cards shift to warm dark surfaces. Blue accent brightens slightly for dark contrast. Remains calm and document-like even in dark mode.

## Best For

Detail-oriented planners, users who prefer calm low-stimulation interfaces, reading-heavy use cases like the schedule and help pages.

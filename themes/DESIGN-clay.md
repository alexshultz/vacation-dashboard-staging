## Overview

Sun-baked terracotta and handmade ceramic. Clay is the most organic theme -- warm, grounded, and textural. Terracotta primary on creamy linen backgrounds with muted sage as the cool counterpoint. Feels like a pottery studio or a Southwestern kitchen rather than a tech product.

## Typography

DM Sans (Google Fonts). Rounder letterforms suit the organic, handmade character of this theme better than the geometric Inter.

## Light Mode

Warm linen page background with cream-white cards. Terracotta primary actions feel grounded and handcrafted. Sage secondary provides natural organic counterpoint. Very warm -- no cool tones in the base palette.

## Dark Mode

Deep warm-brown dark backgrounds (H=20 formula). Terracotta lightens to maintain contrast. Sage remains muted but legible. Feels like a candlelit pottery studio at night.

## Best For

Families who appreciate handmade aesthetics, warm and cozy mood, users who find blue-dominant themes cold.

## Overview

Saturday morning, back door open, sunlight across the Queensland backyard. Bluey uses Bluey's iconic blue heeler fur as the primary color, Bingo's warm orange as the sand accent, and Chloe's fancy purple for committed events. Light mode is a bright sunny backyard day. Dark mode is Bluey and Bingo in their beds after a big day of imagination, the window showing deep navy night sky.

## Colors

- **Primary (#2A6AAA):** Bluey's blue heeler fur -- deep, confident, clear.
- **Secondary (#2A7A1A):** Backyard yard grass green -- confirmed and going!
- **Tertiary (#6030A0):** Chloe's purple -- a bit fancy, a bit locked in.
- **Surface (#FAFEFF / #F0F8FF):** Australian sky-blue white -- clean and bright.
- **Accent sand (#C87030):** Bingo's warm orange coat -- cheerful and energetic.
- **Status neutral (#4A6880):** Slate blue-grey -- undecided, like a Bandit shrug.

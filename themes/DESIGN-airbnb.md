## Overview

Familiar warmth of the Airbnb travel aesthetic. Clean whites with warm coral red and teal-green accents — photography-forward, friendly, optimistic. The palette is deliberately simple: one warm accent, one cool accent, generous white space. Feels like browsing a travel wishlist before a great trip.

## Colors

| Name | Hex | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Rausch Coral | #FF5A5F | Primary action -- Airbnb's signature red-coral |
| Kazan Teal | #008489 | Secondary -- fresh counterpoint to coral |
| Arches Gold | #FFB400 | Tertiary / wishlist accent |
| Clean White | #FFFFFF | Card surface |
| Warm Off-White | #FFF8F6 | Page background -- barely warm |
| Rausch Dark | #C13515 | Error / danger |
| Hof Gray | #717171 | Body text dim / neutral |

## Typography

System fonts only — Airbnb Cereal is not available on Google Fonts. Falls back cleanly to `system-ui` / `Inter` / `sans-serif`.

## Light Mode

Clean photography-first layout. Pure white cards on barely-warm off-white background. Coral primary buttons, teal secondary actions, gold for wishlist and highlights. Generous white space. Text in warm charcoal #333333.

## Dark Mode

Warm near-black background (H=3, dark formula). Coral and teal remain vivid against dark surface. Photography still pops. Gold shifts to a slightly brighter hue for legibility.

## Best For

Photography-forward mood, travel inspiration sessions, users familiar with Airbnb's design language.
